## Personal Development Goals
1. **Skill Acquisition**
  - Complete the data structures and algorithms course by January 2026.
  - Build at least three projects utilizing new skills by June 2026.
2. **Spiritual Growth**
  - Continue the 1 & 2 year Bible reading plans.
  - Continue the intermittent prayer routine.
  - Add study of ROR every interval
  - Study a new book before and after sleeping.
  - Fast every Wednesday and Friday.
  - 3 of intense prayer and fasting quarterly.
  - Night virgil (11pm - 3am) every working day.
3. **Physical Health**
  - Sit-ups: 50 daily in the next 30 days.
  - Push-ups: 20 daily in the next 30 days.
  - Jogging: 1 mile every alternate day for the next 30 days.
4. **Mental Well-being**
  - Meditate for at least 10 minutes daily.
  - Read one self-improvement book every two months.
5. **Social Connections**
  - Reconnect with one old friend or family member each month
  - Attend at least one social or networking event every quarter.
6. **Financial Literacy**
  - Read one book on personal finance every quarter.
  - Track all expenses and savings weekly.
7. **Hobbies and Interests**
  - Dedicate at least 2 hours weekly to a hobby or interest.
  - Learn a new hobby or skill every six months.
8. **Career Development**
  - Attend at least one professional development workshop or seminar every six months.
  - Update resume and LinkedIn profile quarterly.
9. **Time Management**
  - Implement the Pomodoro technique for work and study sessions.
  - Review and adjust daily schedule weekly to optimize productivity.
10. **Travel and Exploration**
  - Plan and take at least one trip to a new destination every year.
  - Explore local attractions or events monthly.
